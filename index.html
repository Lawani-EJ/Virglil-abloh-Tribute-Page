Virgil Abloh

2009-2021

"Life is so short you can't waste even a day subscribing to what someone thinks you can do versus knowing what you can do"

Biography

Virgil Abloh was an American fashion designer and entrepreneur. A trained architect, Abloh founded his own line of luxury streetwear clothing under the moniker Pyrex Vision in 2012, which he transformed into the Milan-based fashion label Off-White in 2013.
